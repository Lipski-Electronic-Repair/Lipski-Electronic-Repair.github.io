Lipski's Electronic Repair


Who we are:

We are a small business located in Washington, Iowa, that specializes in the repair of computers and other consumer electronics.
My name is Joseph Lipski, and I am the owner and lead technician for Lipski's Electronic Repair.
I work alongside my brother, Aidan, to provide excellent customer service and repairs.

What we do:

We are willing to fix just about anything, whether it be phones, computers, or game systems.
We are also able to assist with software issues, networking, equipment installation, and much more.
For more details on our available services, please see our "offered services" page.

Our experience:

Joseph:

I have been opening and repairing computers since the age of 10.
I have experience fixing desktop computer towers, all-in-one systems, laptops, and small servers.
I am able to upgrade or exchange any component in a computer, as long as it is not soldered to the board.
I'm proficient with Microsoft Windows, which allows me to diagnose and fix most software and driver issues.
I also have the ability to repair cell phones, and I have been working on them for around three years.
On top of that, I am also capable of fixing most video game systems, and I also have experience modding them.

Aidan:

I started working on video game systems in mid-2019.
Most of what I work on for game consoles are Xbox 360, Playstation 3, Playstation 4, and Nintendo Wii systems.
I'm also able to fix game controllers, clean out and repaste game consoles to make them run quietly and cooler.
I also have experience working with desktop computers, and I'm confidently able to repair most issues involving them.
